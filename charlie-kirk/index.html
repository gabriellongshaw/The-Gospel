Charlie Kirk

American political activist

Charles James Kirk was a Christian, American conservative political activist, author, and media personality. He co-founded Turning Point USA (TPUSA) in 2012 and later led Turning Point Action. Known for his sharp debates and defense of conservative values, Kirk became one of the most prominent voices of the populist MAGA movement in the Republican Party.

Early Life

Born in Arlington Heights, Illinois, October 14, 1993, Charlie Kirk showed an early interest in political commentary and leadership. At just 18, he founded Turning Point USA.

Turning Point USA

TPUSA became a leading conservative student movement, focused on free markets, limited government, and individual liberty, with a presence in thousands of schools and universities.

Media Presence

Kirk hosted the Charlie Kirk Show and frequently appeared on national television, podcasts, and conferences, reaching millions with his messages.

Legacy

Though controversial, his impact on conservative youth activism is undeniable. His boldness and dedication continue to inspire future generations of leaders.

Death

Charlie Kirk was shot on September 10, 2025 (aged 31) Orem, Utah, U.S.
